What is the D4SD Hub?

D4SD is a regional cooperation platform convened by the Kingdom of Morocco and UNDP, supporting Arab and African countries in advancing digital transformation for sustainable development.

Anchored in Morocco's Digital 2030 Strategy, D4SD catalyzes inclusive digital growth, AI adoption, and South–South cooperation.

The Hub serves as a neutral convener bringing together governments, private sector, academia, and civil society to co-create inclusive digital and AI ecosystems that accelerate progress toward the Sustainable Development Goals.
